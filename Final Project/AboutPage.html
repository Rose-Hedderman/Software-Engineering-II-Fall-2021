Developers: Sebastian Castellanos, Rose Hedderman, Mihir Kamble, Jin Lin, Naho Shioyama

As former highschool graduates, we were once faced with the difficult challange of deciding which university was the right one for each of us.

Our goal is to provide a service that simplifies the confusion and complexity when it comes to universities.
